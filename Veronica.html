Veronica

Veronica is our robot base for the ARF 2024 competition. She has been meticulously reimagined as Veronica 2.0 to enhance both functionality and performance.

Key Features:

  • Large Motors: Two large motors for primary movement.

  • Medium Motors: Two medium motors to power various attachments.

  • Color Sensors: Two color sensors for precise detection and navigation.

  • Stability: Equipped with larger wheels for enhanced stability.

  • Universal Adapter: On top, Veronica shares a universal adapter, allowing for quick attachment changes, similar to the one used by Laura.

  • Trigger Mechanism: The color sensor is triggered by a "T" shaped component in green, which activates Veronica's functions when pushed, as her hub is faced down.
